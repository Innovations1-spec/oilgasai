About OilGasAI

OilGasAI integrates large language models (LLMs), edge AI, and real-time sensor data to help oil and gas operators monitor emissions, predict equipment failure, and automate compliance reporting. The system uses Hugging Face, LangChain, PyTorch, and NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 GPUs, trained on over 1 TB of field and regulatory datasets.

Our Technology

Data Ingestion

Low-cost methane, flow, and temperature sensors send continuous data to edge nodes powered by Raspberry Pi and Jetson devices.

Calibration System

Our simulation and calibration framework replicates field conditions and monitors sensor drift for precise, reliable measurements.

AI + LLM Engine

Custom-trained transformer and CNN models analyze real-time data for predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, and compliance automation.

Deployment

Containerized inference via Docker, FastAPI, and Kubernetes ensures scalable, low-latency AI across cloud and edge environments.
